Team Rules

General Conduct

  • Be respectful and supportive to all team members.
  • No cheating, botting, or exploiting.
  • Follow the Nitro Type Terms of Service at all times.

Race Participation

  • Complete at least 20 races per week to remain active.
  • Participate in team events and competitions when possible.
  • Notify team leaders if you plan to be inactive for an extended period.

Communication

  • Use the official Discord for team communication.
  • Keep discussions positive and avoid conflict.
  • Report any issues or rule violations to team leaders.
